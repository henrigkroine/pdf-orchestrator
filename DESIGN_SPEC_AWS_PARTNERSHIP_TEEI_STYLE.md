## DETAILED PAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### Page 1: TFU Cover (AWS Partnership)

**Background**: Full teal #00393F bleed

**TEEI Logo**:
- Position: Top-left corner
- Margins: 40pt from top and left
- Color: White #FFFFFF
- Size: ~80pt wide (3-bar logo)

**Hero Photo Card**:
- **Image**: Warm, authentic photo showing:
  - Students using technology/laptops (AWS-related context)
  - OR: Refugee students in learning environment
  - OR: Technology education scene with diverse students
- **Dimensions**: 460pt wide × 420pt tall
- **Position**: Centered horizontally, 210pt from top
- **Rounded corners**: 24pt radius
- **Treatment**: No overlay, clean card

**Document Title**:
- **Text**: "Together for Ukraine" (serif, elegant)
- **Font**: Lora Bold
- **Size**: 60pt
- **Color**: White #FFFFFF
- **Position**: Centered, 100pt below photo card
- **Alignment**: Center
- **Letter-spacing**: -0.5pt

**Subtitle**:
- **Text**: "AWS PARTNERSHIP" (all caps)
- **Font**: Roboto Regular
- **Size**: 14pt
- **Case**: ALL CAPS
- **Color**: White #FFFFFF
- **Position**: Centered, 30pt below title
- **Letter-spacing**: 2.5pt (generous tracking)

**Notes**:
- Do NOT use "Empowering Ukrainian Students Through Technology" phrasing
- Do NOT show AWS logo on cover
- Do NOT show metrics on cover
- Keep cover clean and editorial

## TYPOGRAPHY & COLOR REQUIREMENTS

### Typography (STRICT)
- **Serif**: Lora only (Regular, SemiBold, Bold)
- **Sans**: Roboto only (Regular, Medium, Bold)
- **NO**: MinionPro, Arial, Helvetica, or any other fonts

### Colors (STRICT)
- **Teal**: #00393F (RGB 0, 57, 63) - primary
- **White**: #FFFFFF - text on teal
- **Black**: #000000 - text on white
- **Light Blue**: #C9E4EC or #D8EEF5 - stats box
- **Blue (Badge)**: #3D5CA6 - Together for Ukraine badge left
- **Yellow (Badge)**: #FFD500 - Together for Ukraine badge right
- **NO**: Gold #BA8F5A, orange, copper, or any other colors

### Layout (STRICT)
- **Margins**: 40pt all sides
- **Grid**: 12-column, 20pt gutters
- **Spacing**: Follow TFU spacing scale (60pt, 40pt, 32pt, 20pt, 12pt)
- **Rounded corners**: 24pt (cover photo), 8pt (supporting images)
